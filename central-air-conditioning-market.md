<p><strong>The Central Air Conditioning Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Expansion (DX) Central Air Conditioning Systems</li><li>Central Plant/Chilled Water Central Air Conditioning System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The central air conditioning market consists of two main types: Direct Expansion (DX) systems and Central Plant/Chilled Water systems. DX systems use refrigerant to absorb heat directly from the indoor air, providing efficient cooling for residential and commercial spaces. In contrast, Central Plant/Chilled Water systems utilize a centralized chiller to produce chilled water, which is then circulated through ducts or piping to cool multiple areas. Each system has its advantages, depending on the building size, energy efficiency needs, and application.</p></p>
