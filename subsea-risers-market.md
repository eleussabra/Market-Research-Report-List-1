<p><p>Subsea risers are critical for offshore oil and gas operations, categorized into four main types. Production risers transport hydrocarbons from seabed to surface facilities, ensuring efficient extraction. Drilling risers are utilized during the drilling process, connecting the drilling rig to the wellhead, enabling fluid and cuttings removal. Workover risers facilitate maintenance and repair operations on existing wells. Other risers include specialized types for deep-water applications or unique environmental conditions, enhancing the versatility and reliability of subsea interventions.</p></p>
