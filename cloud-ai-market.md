<p><ul><li>Public Clouds</li><li>Private Clouds</li><li>Hybrid Clouds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cloud AI market consists of three main types: Public Clouds, Private Clouds, and Hybrid Clouds. Public Clouds are shared infrastructures provided by third-party vendors, offering scalable resources and services to multiple users. Private Clouds are dedicated environments tailored for a single organization, ensuring enhanced security and control over data. Hybrid Clouds combine both public and private solutions, enabling organizations to leverage the benefits of both models while maintaining flexibility and optimizing costs. Together, they support diverse AI applications and workloads.</p></p>
