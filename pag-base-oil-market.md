<p><p>PAG (Polyalkylene Glycol) base oils are categorized into water-soluble and water-insoluble types, each serving specific applications. Water-soluble PAGs exhibit excellent lubricating properties and are often used in metalworking fluids, offering good emulsion stability and biodegradability. In contrast, water-insoluble PAGs provide superior lubricity and thermal stability, making them ideal for applications like greases and heavy industrial lubricants. Both types are valued for their non-toxic nature and compatibility with various additives, enhancing their versatility in different industries.</p></p>
