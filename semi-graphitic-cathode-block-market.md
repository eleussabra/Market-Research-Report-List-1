<p><p>The semi-graphitic cathode block market is categorized into two primary types: bottom blocks and side blocks. Bottom blocks are designed to serve as the base of the cell, typically offering enhanced thermal and electrical conductivity, which improves overall efficiency. Side blocks, on the other hand, are positioned vertically along the sides of the cell and provide structural stability and effective heat dissipation. Both types are crucial to optimizing performance in industrial applications, primarily in aluminum smelting and other high-temperature processes.</p></p>
