<p><strong>The Transcatheter Mitral Valve Intervention Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li> Restorative Interventional Devices</li><li>Replacement Interventional Devices </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transcatheter Mitral Valve Intervention market is divided into two main types: restorative and replacement interventional devices. Restorative devices aim to repair the existing mitral valve structure, enhancing its function without complete replacement. These include techniques like edge-to-edge repair. In contrast, replacement interventional devices involve the implantation of a new valve, entirely replacing the malfunctioning mitral valve. Both types are designed to improve patient outcomes in those suffering from mitral valve disorders, offering less invasive treatment options compared to traditional surgeries.</p></p>
