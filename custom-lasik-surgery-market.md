<p><ul><li>Wavefront Guided</li><li>Wavefront Optimized</li><li>Topography LASIK</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The custom LASIK surgery market includes three primary types: Wavefront Guided, Wavefront Optimized, and Topography LASIK. Wavefront Guided LASIK utilizes advanced mapping technology to create a highly personalized treatment plan, addressing unique visual imperfections. Wavefront Optimized LASIK is designed to improve overall vision while minimizing post-surgery distortions, using pre-defined parameters. Topography LASIK incorporates detailed corneal maps to customize treatment based on the eye's surface, enhancing accuracy and visual outcomes. These technologies collectively enhance the precision and effectiveness of refractive surgery.</p></p>
