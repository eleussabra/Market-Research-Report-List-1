<p><p>ICP etchers, used in semiconductor manufacturing, operate in two primary market types: open-load and lock-load. Open-load systems allow flexible wafer handling, accommodating various sizes and types, making them suitable for rapid prototyping and smaller production runs. Lock-load systems, on the other hand, feature tightly controlled environments for processing, ensuring enhanced precision and consistency during etching. This makes them ideal for high-volume production where uniformity is critical. Each market type caters to specific production needs and operational efficiencies.</p></p>
