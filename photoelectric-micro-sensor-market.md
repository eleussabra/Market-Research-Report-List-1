<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Photoelectric Micro Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective Photoelectric Micro Sensor</li><li>Diffuse Photoelectric Micro Sensor</li><li>Through Beam Photoelectric Micro Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoelectric micro sensor market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Reflective Photoelectric Micro Sensors** detect objects by measuring the light reflected off them, ideal for short-range applications. </p><p>2. **Diffuse Photoelectric Micro Sensors** operate by emitting light that is reflected from the target, allowing for detection without the need for a separate reflector. </p><p>3. **Through Beam Photoelectric Micro Sensors** consist of a transmitter and receiver that communicate directly when an object interrupts the beam, providing high accuracy for long distances. Each type serves distinct industrial automation needs.</p></p>
