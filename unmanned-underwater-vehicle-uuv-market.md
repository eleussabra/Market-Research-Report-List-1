<p><p>The Unmanned Underwater Vehicle (UUV) market comprises two primary types: Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs) and Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs). ROVs are tethered and controlled from the surface, utilized for tasks like inspection and repair in challenging underwater environments. In contrast, AUVs operate independently, programmed to perform missions such as mapping and data collection without real-time human intervention. Both types are essential in marine exploration, defense, environmental monitoring, and offshore industry applications, driving advancements in underwater technology.</p></p>
