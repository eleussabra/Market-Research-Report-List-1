<p><p>The Water Blocking Swellable Tape market is categorized into three types: Conductive, Semi-Conductive, and Non-Conductive. Conductive tapes facilitate electrical conductivity, making them suitable for applications in environments where grounding is essential. Semi-Conductive tapes provide moderate conductivity, suitable for specific applications that require a balance between insulation and conductivity. Non-Conductive tapes, on the other hand, offer isolation from electrical currents, making them ideal for standard sealing and moisture prevention without conducting electricity, ensuring safety in various applications.</p></p>
