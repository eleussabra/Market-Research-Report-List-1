<p><p>The carbon steel round bar market can be divided into two main types: low carbon steel and high carbon steel. Conversely, high carbon steel, with carbon content between 0.6% to 1.4%, offers increased hardness and strength, suitable for tools, automotive components, and high-stress applications. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, driving market demand.</p></p>
