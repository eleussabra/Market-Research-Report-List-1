<p><p>The veterinary weighing scale market comprises two primary types: electronic and mechanical weighing scales. Electronic veterinary weighing scales utilize digital technology to provide accurate weight measurements, often featuring advanced functions such as data storage, connectivity, and user-friendly displays. In contrast, mechanical veterinary weighing scales operate through traditional methods, using springs and levers to measure weight without batteries or electricity. Both types serve vital roles in animal healthcare, enabling precise monitoring of animal weight for health assessments and treatment planning.</p></p>
