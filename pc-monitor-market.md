<p><strong>The PC Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LCD</li><li>OLED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PC monitor market comprises primarily two types: LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) and OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode). LCD monitors utilize liquid crystals and a backlight to produce images, offering energy efficiency and affordability, making them popular for general use. In contrast, OLED monitors feature self-emissive pixels that deliver superior color accuracy, contrast, and faster response times. While LCDs dominate the market due to their lower cost, OLEDs are gaining traction, particularly among professionals seeking high-quality visual experiences.</p></p>
