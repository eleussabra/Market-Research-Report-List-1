<p><p>The stroke treatment drugs market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Anticoagulants** prevent blood clot formation, reducing the risk of ischemic strokes.</p><p>2. **Statins** lower cholesterol levels and stabilize artery plaques, minimizing stroke risk.</p><p>3. **Tissue Plasminogen Activator (tPA)** is a thrombolytic agent that dissolves clots during an acute ischemic stroke treatment window.</p><p>4. **Antiplatelet drugs** inhibit platelet aggregation, preventing thrombus formation in patients at risk, particularly in those with a history of transient ischemic attacks.</p></p>
