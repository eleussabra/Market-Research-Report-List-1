<p><p>The Load Bank Sale and Rental market encompasses three key types: Resistive Load Banks, Reactive Load Banks, and Resistive/Reactive Load Banks. Resistive Load Banks are designed to simulate electrical loads, primarily using resistive elements to test power sources under steady conditions. Reactive Load Banks incorporate inductive or capacitive components to mimic real-world conditions, testing generators more realistically. Resistive/Reactive Load Banks combine both resistive and reactive elements, providing versatile testing environments that challenge power systems across a range of applications.</p></p>
