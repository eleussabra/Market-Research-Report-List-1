<p><strong>The PET Preform Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Crystallized Neck Finish Preforms</li><li>Crystallized Neck Finish Preforms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET preform market is primarily divided into two types: non-crystallized neck finish preforms and crystallized neck finish preforms. Non-crystallized neck finish preforms are designed for applications requiring standard neck finishes and are suitable for immediate bottling. In contrast, crystallized neck finish preforms undergo a process that enhances their thermal resistance, making them ideal for high-temperature applications like hot-filling. Each type serves distinct packaging needs, impacting industries like beverages, food, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
