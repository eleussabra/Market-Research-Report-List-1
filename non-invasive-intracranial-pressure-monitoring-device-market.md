<p><p>The non-invasive intracranial pressure monitoring device market comprises several techniques used to assess intracranial pressure without direct access to the skull. Transcranial Doppler Ultrasonography evaluates blood flow velocity in brain vessels; Tympanic Membrane Displacement measures pressure changes via the ear; Optic Nerve Sheath Diameter assesses nerve sheath enlargement via ultrasound; MRI/CT imaging provides detailed brain scans for indirect pressure assessment; and Fundoscopy identifies papilledema, reflecting increased intracranial pressure through examination of the eye's retina.</p></p>
