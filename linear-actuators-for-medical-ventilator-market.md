<p><p>Linear actuators for medical ventilators are essential components that convert energy into precise linear motion, crucial for patient ventilation. The two primary types are pneumatic and electric actuators. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air to generate movement, offering rapid response and reliability, ideal for emergency applications. In contrast, electric actuators provide precise control and are quieter, making them suitable for continuous monitoring in critical care. Both types are vital for ensuring effective ventilation and improved patient outcomes in healthcare settings.</p></p>
