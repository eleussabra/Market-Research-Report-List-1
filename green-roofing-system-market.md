<p><p>The green roofing system market comprises three main types: intensive, extensive, and others. Intensive green roofs are characterized by deeper soil media, supporting larger plants and greater biodiversity, often requiring significant structural support and maintenance. Extensive green roofs feature shallower soils and low-growing vegetation, making them lightweight and easier to maintain, ideal for urban settings. Other types may include modular systems or biophilic designs, offering flexibility and customization for various applications, enhancing aesthetics and promoting sustainability in urban environments.</p></p>
