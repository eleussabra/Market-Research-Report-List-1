<p><p>The digital color sensor market is segmented into three main types: RGB, NIR, and others. RGB sensors capture red, green, and blue light, enabling accurate color detection for applications in displays and cameras. NIR (Near Infrared) sensors measure infrared light, useful in agriculture and medical diagnostics. The "others" category includes specialized sensors like multispectral and hyperspectral sensors that analyze various wavelengths for advanced applications such as environmental monitoring, industrial inspection, and security systems. Each type serves distinct market needs.</p></p>
