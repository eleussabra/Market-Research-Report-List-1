<p><strong>The Pigment Dispersant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nonionic Type</li><li>Cationic Type</li><li>Anionic Type</li><li>Amphoteric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pigment dispersant market comprises several types based on their ionic properties. Nonionic types exhibit neutrality, making them versatile for various applications without affecting the pigment's charge. Cationic types possess a positive charge, enhancing adhesion in specific formulations like coatings and adhesives. Anionic types carry a negative charge, providing stability and improved dispersion in water-based systems. Amphoteric types, with both positive and negative charges, offer flexibility in diverse formulations, catering to varying pH levels and improving compatibility with different pigments.</p></p>
