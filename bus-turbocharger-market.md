<p><strong>The Bus Turbocharger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mono Turbo</li><li>Twin Turbo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bus turbocharger market comprises two main types: mono turbo and twin turbo systems. Mono turbo systems use a single turbocharger to compress air for the engine, offering simplicity and efficiency for standard performance needs. In contrast, twin turbo systems feature two turbochargers, optimizing power delivery and enhancing responsiveness across a broader RPM range. This dual setup allows for improved acceleration and efficiency, making it particularly appealing for high-performance buses or those requiring greater power under varying load conditions.</p></p>
