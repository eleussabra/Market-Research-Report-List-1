<p><p>The capacitive inclinometers market is divided into two main types: single-axis and dual-axis inclinometers. Single-axis capacitive inclinometers measure tilt or inclination in one direction, making them suitable for applications like structural monitoring and leveling. In contrast, dual-axis capacitive inclinometers can measure tilt in both horizontal and vertical planes, offering more comprehensive data, which is ideal for applications in civil engineering, aerospace, and robotics. Both types leverage capacitive technology for high precision and reliability in measurement.</p></p>
