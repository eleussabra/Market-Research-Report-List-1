<p><p>The Medical Electromechanical Switch market encompasses various types of switches that facilitate user interaction with medical devices. Tactile switches provide feedback through a noticeable feel, while rotary switches allow users to select from multiple options through rotation. Encoders convert rotational position into an electronic signal. Toggle switches enable straightforward on/off operation, and push switches activate functions with a press. Detection switches sense physical presence, while micro and DIP switches fit compact spaces. Other types include power, slide, and joystick switches tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
