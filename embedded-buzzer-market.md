<p><strong>The Embedded Buzzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Electromagnetic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The embedded buzzer market comprises two main types: piezoelectric and electromagnetic. Piezoelectric buzzers utilize piezoelectric materials to produce sound when an electric current is applied, offering compact size and low power consumption, making them ideal for portable devices. In contrast, electromagnetic buzzers utilize a magnetic field to create sound, providing a louder output but generally requiring more power. Both types are essential in various applications, such as consumer electronics, alarms, and automotive systems, catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
