<p><p>The Pure Epoxy Power Coatings market encompasses two primary types: powder and particles. Powder coatings consist of dry resin particles that, when applied, undergo a curing process upon heat exposure, forming a durable, protective layer suitable for various substrates. Particle coatings, on the other hand, refer to a broader range of particulate materials, including micro or nano-sized epoxy particles, utilized for specialized applications such as improved surface finish, enhanced corrosion resistance, and tailored mechanical properties in coatings. Both types are valued for their eco-friendliness and performance.</p></p>
