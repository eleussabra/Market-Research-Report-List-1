<p><p>Radioactive waste disposal solutions are categorized into three main types: Low-Level Waste (LLW), Medium-Level Waste (MLW), and High-Level Waste (HLW). LLW includes items like contaminated clothing and equipment, requiring less stringent disposal methods. MLW encompasses materials that emit higher radiation levels and necessitate more robust shielding and management. HLW consists of highly radioactive substances from nuclear reactors, needing deep geological storage and advanced engineering solutions due to their long-lived isotopes and extreme hazard potential. Each type demands tailored disposal strategies.</p></p>
