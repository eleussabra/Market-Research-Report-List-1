<p><p>The commercial cyber insurance market consists of two primary types: stand-alone cyber insurance and packaged cyber insurance. Stand-alone cyber insurance offers coverage specifically for cyber-related risks, allowing businesses to tailor their policies to address unique needs such as data breaches, ransomware, and business interruptions. Packaged cyber insurance, on the other hand, integrates cyber coverage with other insurance products, providing a more comprehensive solution that includes general liability or property insurance in a single policy, simplifying risk management for businesses.</p></p>
