<p><strong>The CMP Slurries for Through Silicon Via Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Side Slurries</li><li>Back Side Slurries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMP slurries for Through Silicon Via (TSV) applications are categorized into front side and back side slurries. Front side slurries are used during the chemical-mechanical polishing of the wafer's top surface, enabling precise patterning and integration of TSVs. In contrast, back side slurries are utilized after the wafer is thinned from the backside to improve the surface quality of the TSVs and ensure proper electrical connectivity. Both types are essential for optimizing TSV fabrication processes in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
